SCORE = FACTION SCORE + PIRATE NATION SCORE

The Underworld Boss represents a nation state's "alternative members".
Those who seek power outside of the sanctioned hierarchy of power. They can be
union leaders, smugglers, activists, pirates, outlaws or any other fridge group
players can image.

The Underworld Boss starts the game with 10 credits and 1 sabotage card.

At the beginning of each turn, the The Underworld Bosses for each nation meet together
to form the Underworld NATION council. For each structure in the game, the Council generates
one sabotage per turn. The council, can then use these points to buy sabotage cards.
How these points are spent is decided by simple majority vote underworld bosses may
make any deals to garner votes from other bosses they wish.

Once purchased, the council then decides which member of the council to give the
sabotage cards too. That member can then choose to keep, use, give or sell their sabotage
cards too any other player as they see fit. Furthermore, after a sabotage card is placed,
the placer can entertain bids for credits to have the card have no effect or even change
targets before it goes off. Removing cards in this manner uses up the card.

Each sabotage card costs 5 sabotage points and can be used to:

  • Shutdown a structure for 1 turn
  • Render a ship ,in orbit, immobile for a turn
  • For 2 cards: Cause a single ship to become a pirate ship and be placed under the control
    of the card user (does not need to be underworld boss)
  • For 2 cards: Cause a group of space marines to become outlaws under control of the card user
    (does not need to be underworld boss)

Pirate and outlaw units are hostile to all non-pirate and outlaws; even if controlled by the same player.
Pirate and outlaw units may attack other units without any direct mechanical political consequences.
The identity of the pirate and outlaw unit controller is always hidden


THE PIRATE NATION SCORES 1 VP PER PIRATE SHIP AND PIRATE BASE


Underworld Boss players can spend any credits they make to build units like any other faction.
Like other members, they must contract with the captain of industry to build their
items. Any science they buy becomes researched by the chief scientist.

Any unit built buy the Boss becomes a pirate/outlaws



People's Revolution: For a cost of 20 credits, the Underworld Boss can have any deployed structure
in play, revolt and come under that Boss's control. Doing so, atomically generates 1 space marine
for the player. Any hostile space marines on board the structure immediately fight the structure for control.
Underworld structures convert all other faction points into credits and generate 2 VPs for the pirate nation

Any units that come under the control of the Underworld Boss become Pirate Nation units.

Pirate Units:

Pirate units are automatically hostile to all other units
except other pirate units. Even units from the same nation as the Boss are hostile
to pirate units. Pirate units may attack and be attacked without causing any negative
mechanical political consequences.

Civil War


Team mates ,other than the Head of State, can attack each other. Should that happen,
the nation enters into a state of civil war. During a civil war, the incomes of all team
members are halved. Civil wars continue until either the attacked or the attacker is driven
from the map.

Attacking pirate units or outlaw units under the control of your team mates
does not start a civil war.

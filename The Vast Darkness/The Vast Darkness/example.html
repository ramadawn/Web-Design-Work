Players performance

Individually the Chief Scientist gains one VP per science

station and technology the have. The Captain of Industry gains one VP per mining colony built as well as points for building Great Projects.
Finally, the Head of State gains 2 VP per colony founded and 1 vp per advancement in interplanetary government they research.

Great Projects:


Cost = 50 credits

CV = 9


  • Great Projects represent massive leaps in the conquest of space.
  • Great projects are built by the Capitan Of Industry at a cost of 50 credits each.
  • Once built, they benefit all members of a nation.
  • Unlike other structures, Great Projects occupy no space on the map.
  • Each nation can only build one of each Great Project.
  • Planet Specific Projects require a full colony near by.
  • Ceres project only requires a mining colony on Ceres.
  • Great Projects gain 10 victory points at the end of the game for the Capitan Of Industry.

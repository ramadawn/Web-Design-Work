The Vast Darkness


Welcome to the Vast Darkness


The Vast Darkness is a game that simulates the initial
space race to colonize the solar system after the
invention of the fusion drive.

Each player represents separate factions within a
one of the initial space faring nation states

The Faction Heads:


The Head Of State

The Chief Scientist

Capitan Of Industry

Underworld Boss

UN Representative






Game Objective:

The goal of each player in the Vast Darkness is to Simultaneously maximize their team's score and their personal score.
Teams maximize their score by controlling the largest portion of the solar system by the end of the game and
players maximize their personal score by achieving their own faction objectives. The sum of these two scores
forms a player's total score.


Game Phases:

The game is divided up in several phases:

Purchase and Meeting Phase: Meeting are held
Things are bought and sold
Mission Phase: Missions (other than 1G and High G) are paid for and launched,
At the end of this phase all active missions are shown.
Reaction Phase: 1G and HIGH G missions may be launched.
They arrive during the arrival phase
On Planet actions: Military units on a body at the beginning of the turn may act
Arrival Phase: Missions that are scheduled to arrive; arrive.
Those missions may now take actions, such as land of operate or attack.
If multiple missions arrive at the same place the players take actions in order
of initiative.

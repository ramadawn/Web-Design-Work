Mining Colonies:


Cost = 4 credits

CV = 5


Mining Colonies are home to temporary workers focused on making the most money .
Mining colonies typically fall under the control of the Captain Of Industry
.
Unlike full colonies they offer no special benefits.
Each turn, mining colonies produce an amount of credits as determined by the table below.
The credits and policy points collected, go to the Captain Of Industry
.


Unless the colony has been taken over by the Underworld Boss.

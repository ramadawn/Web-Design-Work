Policy Cards:

Policy Cards cost 5 Government Policy point each


Policy Name Description
Secondary Competitors: Build 1 item without use of Captain of Industry
Duplicate Production: Build 1 item and get a second one for free
Excellent Logistics: For one turn of combat, all ships fire with a +1
Government intelligence: Force Chief Scientist to show you his hand.
Covert Strike: Make an attack without declaration of war.
Internal Purge: Make an attack against another member of your nation.
Tax Raid: Claim 10 credits from another player on your team.
Covert Operations: Steal 5 credits from another player not on your team

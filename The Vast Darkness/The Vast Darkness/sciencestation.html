Science Stations:


Cost = 6 credits

CV = 9


Science Stations are orbital homes to cutting edge research.
Within their hulls, the best and brightest minds of Earth live and breath science.
Science Stations typically fall under the control of the The Chief Scientist.
Unlike other structures, science stations sit in orbit and thus occupy no space on the map.
Each nation can have multiple science stations in orbit
Each turn, science stations produce an amount of science as determined by the table below.
These science points can be converted into credits on a one to one basis by the The Chief Scientist
The science points collected, go to the Chief Scientist.


Unless the station has been taken over by the Underworld Boss.

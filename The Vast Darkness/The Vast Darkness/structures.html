Space Structures:

Space structures form the backbone of power projection in the Vast Darkness.
It is through structures that players claim space, generate additional income,
claim victory points and develop research and government policies.

All structures have a base 12 hit points. These hit points can either be captured
(in order to capture the structure) or destroyed (in order to destroy it).
All structures can house an unlimited amount of space marines. Friendly ships in
proximity of a structure can repair 1 hit point of damage for the cost of one credit.


There are four types of Structures in the game:


Full Colony

Mining Colony

Science Station

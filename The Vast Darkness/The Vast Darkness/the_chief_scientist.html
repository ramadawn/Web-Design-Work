The Chief Scientist


SCORE = FACTION SCORE + 1VP PER FACTION CONTROLLED SCIENCE STATION + 1VP PER TECHNOLOGY CARD IN THEIR HAND

The Chief Scientist represents the state's educated elite.
The chief scientist starts the game with a one-time amount of 20 credits. After that,
any additional funds must come from other players, research stations within
their faction, or the sale of any technology they control.

Any new technology cards researched or bought by a nation, are placed in the hands of the
chief scientist. Once researched, all players within a faction benefit from the technology cards.
That said, in order for a player to benefit from a technology card, he/she must know that their
chief scientist owns a card.

The Chief scientist is NOT obligated to show the other players in their
nation the science cards in their hand; and may even demand things
in exchange for doing so.


(NOTE: Should another faction member acquire a technology card before the chief scientist,
the chief scientist automatically gains that technology card)

(NOTE: Only the chief scientist may sell away their technology cards)
